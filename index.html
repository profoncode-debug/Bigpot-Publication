About the Press

A printing press built for the railway aspirant.

Bigpot Publication is an independent printing press. We write, typeset, print and bind every title under one roof — which means our books reach you faster, cost less, and stay current with each new edition and amendment.

Our focus is narrow on purpose: Railway Protection Force examinations and Railway Engineering training. From the Railways Act and the new criminal codes to Carriage & Wagon stream theory, every page is prepared to match the official syllabus.
